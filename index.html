Modern Data Platforms: Powering the Next Wave of Analytics and AI

Modern analytics and AI depend on more than powerful models. They depend on modern data platforms. In fact, recent DBTA research shows that 85% of subscribers are planning to modernize their data platforms, fueled by the rapid growth of GenAI and LLMs.

As organizations race to support AI workloads, real-time analytics, and scalable self-service, legacy architectures are quickly becoming a bottleneck. Data platforms must now be faster, more flexible, and built to support analytics and AI side by side, serving as the foundation for innovation, performance, and scale.

To explore how modern data platforms are reshaping the way organizations store, manage, and activate data, DBTA is bringing together industry experts for a special roundtable webinar.

Join this interactive discussion to learn how enterprises are:

  • Modernizing legacy platforms for AI and advanced analytics
  • Unifying data across cloud, on-prem, and hybrid environments
  • Supporting AI workloads alongside analytics and operational systems
  • Enabling faster, more flexible data engineering and pipelines
